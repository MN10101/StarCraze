What is Astrology?

Astrology is a belief system that suggests a connection between the positions and movements of celestial bodies, such as planets and stars, and events on Earth, including human life and personality. It is often divided into different branches, with horoscopic astrology being one of the most well-known. In horoscopic astrology, an individual's birth chart, based on the positions of celestial bodies at the time of their birth, is used to interpret and predict various aspects of their life, personality, and destiny. While astrology has cultural and historical significance, it's important to note that it is not scientifically proven, and its principles are not universally accepted..
